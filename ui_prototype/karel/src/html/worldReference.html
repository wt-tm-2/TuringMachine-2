Building the World Interactively

There are two different ways to construct the world in Karel the Robot. The easiest way is to build it interactively by clicking inside the world grid.


Adding and Removing Walls

Left clicking between rooms allows you add walls between those rooms. Walls can be removed by left clicking again after they are placed.


Adding and Removing Beepers

Right click in the center of a room to place a beeper stack. You can continue to right click on the stack to increment the number of beepers in the stack. Each time you left click on a beeper stack, the number of beepers in the stack is decremented until the beeper stack is completely removed.


Building the World Manually

Another way to construct the world is through the world editor. The world editor allows a little bit more control over the world by allowing you to choose the starting location of the robot. You can also change the amount of beepers the robot starts off with in its beeper bag.


The World Editor

Each world editor instruction must be on a separate line. There are three possible instructions that allow you to place walls, beeper stacks, and change the parameters regarding the robot. For each instruction you specify the position in the grid of the room. When you place a beeper stack, you must specify the number of beepers in the stack. When you specify a wall or the robot, you must give a direction of N E S W to give the direction the wall or robot faces. For the robot, you also give the number of beepers in the robot's beeper bag.


World Editor Instructions

The instructions take the following form: 

robot <horizontal position> <vertical position> <direction facing> <beepers in bag>

wall <horizontal position> <vertical position> <direction facing>

beeper <horizontal position> <vertical position> <number of beepers in stack>

The Load World button is used to construct the world from the instructions inside the world editor.
